Mozzarella Stuffed Meatballs

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 14
Ingredients for Meatballs Quantity Ingredients for Sauce Quantity
Beef 1 pound Crushed tomato 7 cups
Mild sausage 1 pound Chopped onion 1/3 cup
Breadcrumbs 1 cup Garlic cloves 2
Chopped onion 1/3 cup Dried basil 2 tsp
Parmesan cheese 1/4 cup Dried oregeno 1 tsp
Onion powder 1 tsp Pepper 1/2 tsp
Garlic powder 1 tsp Salt 1/2 tsp
Dried thyme 1 tsp
Dried oregeno 1 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Pepper 1/2 tsp
Eggs 2
Mozzarella cheese 4 sticks
Whole milk 1/2 cup
Parsley 1/2 cup

Step 1: Combine all sauce ingredients in slow cooker and stir. Set to high and cover while making meatballs, or for 30 minutes.
Step 2: Combine all meatball ingredients except the mozzerella in a large bowl. Usiing your hands, mix until fully combined.
Step 3: Take a golf ball sized peice of the ground beef mixture and place a piece of mozzarella in the middle. Press meat around the piece of cheese, fully enclosing it. Repeat until all meat is used.
Step 4: Place meatballs in slow cooker and submerge it in sauce.
Step 5: Cover and cook on high for 2-2 1/2 hours, or until meat is fully cooked.

Chicken bacon ranch mini calzone

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients for calzone Quantity
Rotisserie chicken 1/2, shredded
Pizza dough 1
Ranch packet 1
Red onion 1, thinly sliced
Cooked bacon 1/2 cup, chopped
Mozzarella cheese 4 slices, cut in half
Cheddar cheese 1/2 cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
Step 2: Cut pizza dough into 8 sections on a floured surface.
Step 3: With one section, roll out dough until flat and cirular.
Step 4: In a medium bowl, mix together the chicken, ranch packet, red onion, and bacon.
Step 5: Place a slice of mozzerella on the rolled out dough. Top with 2-3 tablespoons of the chicken mixture.
Step 6: Fold one side of the dough up and over ingredients and seal calzone by crimping the edges together.
Step 7: Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Apply egg wash to outside of calzone and sprinkle with cheddar cheese.
Step 8: Bake calzones at 400 degrees F for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.

Halloween Buffalo Chicken Dip

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantitiy
Cooked chicken 2 cups, shredded
Cream cheese 8 oz, softened
Monterey jack cheese 1/2 cup
Cheddar cheese 2/3 cup
Buffalo sauce 1/2 cup
Ranch dressing 1/2 cup
Pizza dough 2 tubes
Cheddar cheese 4 oz, cubed
Butter 2 tablespoons, melted

Step 1: Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Step 2: In a bowl, combine chicken, cream cheese, monterey jack, buffalo sauce, and ranch.
Step 3: On a slightly floured surface, roll out 1 tube of pizza dough. Cut out triangles for eyes, and a mouth (for the pumpkin face).
Step 4: Bake for 12 minutes, or until the pieces are golden brown, slightly risen and cooked through.
Step 5: Cut the remaining dough into squares, place a cube of cheese on each one, and roll into balls. Repeat with the other tube of pizza dough. Place in a ring around the edges of a greased oven-safe skilled or dish.
Step 6: Fill the middle of the dish with the dip filling.
Step 7: Bake for 35 minutes.
Step 8: Coat all of the dough with melted butter. Place the dough face pieces onto the dip.

Source: Tasty
